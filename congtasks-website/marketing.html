Project #1

AMAN CAFE

A Long-Term Journey

AMAN CAFE is a project I have been closely involved with since 2020 - from its very first days on the market until now. Over more than 4 years, the brand has gone through two major rebrandings, evolving from "Aman Cafe - make you happy" to "Aman Cafe - Halong Base", marking a new stage of growth rooted in the memories and culture of Ha Long.

My role covered brand strategy, visual & content direction, menu development, communication, and community events, helping AMAN become not just a café, but truly a "second home" for its customers.

Project #5

GU COFFEE - HẠ LONG

A delightful small café in Ha Long, I decided to participate in the project due to the trust and familiarity between myself and the founder.

GOALS

  • Designing a brand identity package.
  • Crafting a slogan that captures the café's vibes.
  • Organizing the grand opening event.
  • Sustaining social media activities thereafter.

SOLUTIONS

  • Given the name Gu Coffee, we envision a café with a distinctive taste - something out of the ordinary. At the time, Ha Long lacked any cafes with a black-themed ambiance. Hence, black became the dominant color for Gu, reflecting the founder's preference.
  • Slogan: "GU is we 'G'ot yo'U" - playing with words creatively.
  • Gu's visual identity features a rock-water motif, inspired by Ha Long's unique landscape of sea and mountains.
  • Booking review.
  • Use Gu Coffee's current customers as the main faces of the café.

Project #7

YONGKANG HOTPOT & DIMSUM TAIPEI

YongKang is a project under An Khanh Company, specializing in FnB setup, where I served as the marketing leader.

GOALS

  • Building upon the founder's intrinsic potential, notably their connections with celebrities, the project encountered no obstacles with us.
  • After the restaurant's grand opening, Hanoi experienced a period of social distancing, leading to the closure of the restaurant. Consequently, it was a challenging time for operations, necessitating a complete transition to online platforms.
  • Our goal is to expand online product sales and increase brand recognition.

SOLUTIONS

  • Working together with the restaurant's kitchen team to develop suitable packaging and pricing strategies for executing the online sales plan for hotpot and dimsum.
  • Taking photos, designing visually appealing images to attract viewers.
  • Training the on-page staff team to respond to and close sales as quickly as possible.
