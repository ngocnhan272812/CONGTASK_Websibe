MIAS MENU - TASTE THE CULTURE

In the heart of the Old Quarter, where travelers from around the world gather, the menu was envisioned not just as a list of dishes, but as a culinary magazine - a journey into Vietnam's regional flavors and culture. Beyond standard food photography, our team brought in food maps and concept art, turning opening and waiting pages into moments of curiosity and delight.

Our regular client AMAN CAFE

rising from the culture

Inspired by 'HaLong Base', the menu of Aman Café & Restaurant is designed like a magazine about HaLong – where each page not only presents the dishes but also unfolds cultural glimpses, stories, and iconic images of this heritage land.

Lấy cảm hứng từ âm thanh /si:/ vang như tiếng sóng, concept 'see (sea) là mê' của Aman Café mang đến tinh thần mùa hè rực rỡ – nơi biển xanh, cát vàng và hương vị tươi mát hoà quyện thành trải nghiệm khó quên.
